Password Security

What is "Password Security"?

Password security is a cornerstone feature of cybersecurity and often is the first line of defence against malicious actors. Oftentimes if a user does not have a strong enough password, their data can easily be compromised and can cause large amounts of losses for the user.

What makes a "secure" password:

  • Character length, usually 8 characters or more
  • Use a mix of capitals and lowercase
  • Use a variety of numbers and symbols
  • Don't use identifying information such as your name, birthday, or other easily found information
  • Don't reuse your passwords
